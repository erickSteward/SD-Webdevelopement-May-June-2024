In a world of tags and stylesheets bright,
            HTML and CSS dance in the light.
            HTML, the skeleton, structure defined,
            With elements nested, beautifully aligned.
        
            <!DOCTYPE html> to start the show,
        
            Head, body, divs, oh the places they'll go!
            Paragraphs, headings, lists galore,
            Anchors and images, begging to explore.
        
            But wait! Here comes CSS, with flair,
            To paint the canvas, to style with care.
            Selectors and properties, a symphony they weave,
            Color, font, layout, to make the page breathe.
        
            From margins to paddings, floats and grids,
            Flexbox and grids, where design bids.
            Media queries, responsive and bold,
            To adapt and fit, as screens unfold.
        
            So hand in hand, HTML and CSS stride,
            Creating wonders, where creativity can't hide.
            Basics they may be, but oh, what power they hold,
            In the world wide web, where stories are told.
